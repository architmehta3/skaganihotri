Your Peaceful Retreat in Ujjain


Nestled in the spiritual heart of Ujjain, Shree Shyam Yatri Grah offers a serene and comfortable stay for pilgrims and travelers alike. Our accommodations provide the perfect blend of convenience, comfort, and affordability, making your stay in Ujjain truly memorable.

Explore Ujjain’s Sacred Sites
with Our Ujjain Darshan Tour


Discover the spiritual essence of Ujjain, one of India's holiest cities, with our expertly curated Ujjain Darshan tour. Whether you're a first-time visitor or a returning pilgrim, our tour service offers an immersive and enriching experience of Ujjain's most revered sites.

  • • Comprehensive Itinerary: Visit all the major spiritual landmarks, including the Mahakaleshwar Temple (one of the 12 Jyotirlingas), Kal Bhairav Temple, Ram Ghat (on the sacred Shipra River), Harsiddhi Temple, Chintaman Ganesh Temple, and Bade Ganeshji Ka Mandir.
  • • Expert Guides: Our knowledgeable and friendly guides will provide you with fascinating insights into the history, mythology, and significance of each site, enhancing your spiritual journey.
  • • Comfortable Transportation: Enjoy hassle-free travel in our comfortable, air-conditioned vehicles, ensuring a smooth and relaxing experience as you move between sacred sites.
  • • Flexible Timings: We offer both full-day and half-day tour options, allowing you to choose a schedule that best fits your plans.
  • • Personalized Experience: Customize your tour according to your preferences, with options to focus on specific temples or spiritual practices that are most meaningful to you.
  • • Spiritual Guidance: Get assistance with booking special pujas, Rudrabhishek, or other religious ceremonies at Mahakaleshwar and other temples, ensuring a deeply fulfilling spiritual experience.
  • • Affordable Pricing: Our Ujjain Darshan tours start at just ₹1,500 per person for a half-day tour, making it accessible for all devotees and travelers.
  • • Special Add-ons: Enhance your tour with options like Shree Shyam Yatri Grah stay packages, meal plans at traditional vegetarian restaurants, and souvenir shopping stops at local markets.
